This will increase the users investment at the same time increasing the daily yield earnings. This strategy has already been tried and tested by several project and is proven effective. High Risk! Funds that are used to initially hire farmers (including re-hire) cannot be withdrawn, however your farmers will indefinitely work and grow crops for you. Please use the application at your own risk.
